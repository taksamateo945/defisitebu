Eva Thomas Education Details

Eva joined the University of Idaho for her bachelor’s level education. Eventually, she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Family and Consumer Sciences from there in 2012.

Eventually, Eva completed her Juris of Doctor degree at Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law, Yeshiva University with cum laude in 2019. There, she also received the Paris Baldacci Scholarship and Dean’s Scholarship.

Moreover, Eva was a semifinalist at the 2018 National Latino/Law Student Association Moot Court Competition. She was an octofinalist and third-best oralist in the 2018 Federal Bar Association Thurgood Marshall Moot Court Competition.

What Is Eva Thomas Job?

Eva is an attorney by profession. As of 2023, she is serving as an associate at A.Y. Strauss in the firm’s Litigation Group. A. Y. Strauss law firm is based in Roseland, New Jersey.

Eva currently serves at A. Y. Strauss as an associate (Source: A. Y. Strauss’ Official Website)

Prior to joining A. Y. Strauss, Eva worked as a public defender in Nassau County, New York. Moreover, she was a member of the Civil Rights Clinic at the Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law. Working there, Eva handled several federal litigation matters.

So far, Eva has expertise in breach of contract claims, product liability class actions, business torts, and securities derivative actions. Likewise, she holds experience in the dissolution of LLCs, landlord-tenant disputes, estate litigation, and labor and employment disputes.

Apart from that, Eva is also a supporter of the Real Estate Group with legal research, due diligence, and litigation services. She has appeared in over 500 courts before judges in both civil and criminal matters.

Eva is affiliated with New York State Bar Association.
