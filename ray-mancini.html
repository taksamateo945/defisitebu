Who is Ray Mancini:

Ray Mancini is a famous Boxer. He was born on March 4, 1961 and his birthplace is Youngstown, OH. Ray is also well known as, Boxer who won the Lightweight division championship and is referred to by the nickname ‘Boom Boom.’ He compiled a career record of 29-5.

Ray is originated from United States. He was defeated by Hector Camacho in a controversial split decision finish in March, 1989.
