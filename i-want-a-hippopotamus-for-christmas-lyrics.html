I Want a Hippopotamus for Christmas is a Christmas novelty song that was written by Jon Rox in 1953 and became famous after being performed by then 10-year-old Gayla Peevey.

The song was released in 1953 and quickly became a holiday hit, reaching the top 40 charts.

Gayla Peevey, the singer of the song, became an overnight sensation and continued to perform and record music for several years after the release of the song
