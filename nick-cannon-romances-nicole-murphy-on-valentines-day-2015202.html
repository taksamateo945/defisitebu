Nick Cannon Romances Nicole Murphy on Valentine's Day

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  296 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

No more Mimi! Nick Cannon has been spending his free time with Michael Strahan’s ex-fiancée Nicole Murphy. The America’s Got Talent host, who filed for divorce from Mariah Carey last month, has been romancing Eddie Murphy’s ex-wife, sources reveal to Us Weekly.

On Tuesday, Feb. 10, the pair enjoyed each other’s company over dinner at NYC’s Bagatelle. According to an eyewitness, the duo dined in the back of the restaurant with another couple and “were dressed up and laughing a lot together.” 

Fast-forward four days later, and Cannon was romancing Murphy over dinner on Valentine’s Day uptown.

"Nick and Nicole had a late night romantic dinner on Valentine's Day at Tavern on the Green Saturday night,” another source reveals to Us, adding that the couple showed up to the central-park-adjacent restaurant late around 11 p.m. when most of the other diners had left.

Cannon, 33, who shares 3-year-old twins Moroccan and Monroe with Carey, was so attentive to Murphy, 47, he didn’t even notice when a Carey song came on, the insider tells Us.

“They were too into each other to pay attention — they held hands from across the table gazing at each other throughout their meal,” the eyewitness adds.
