Kristin Smart Parents and Family

Kristin Smart was born on February 20, 1977, in Augsburg, Bavaria, West Germany, to teachers Stan and Denise Smart. They have a total of three children two daughters and a son. Stan Smart and Denise Smart were the recipients of the Key to the City, in honor of their daughter, they struggled for decades for their daughter Kristin’s justice.

Kristin Smart graduated from Lincoln High School in Stockton and attended California Polytechnic State University in 1995. when she was under graduation she disappeared in 1996 after coming back to her hostel.
