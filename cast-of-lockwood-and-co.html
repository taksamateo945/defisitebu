Meet The Cast Of Lockwood And Co. And Learn In Details About Them!

 Posted on March 31, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  994 words  |  Martina Birk

Casts of Lockwood and co include; Lucy Carlyle as Ruby Stokes, Cameron Chapman as Anthony Lockwood, Ivanno Jeremiah as Inspector Barnes, Ali Hadji-Heshmati as George Karim, Jack Bandeira as Quill Kipps, Hayley Konadu as Flo Bones, Morven Christie as Penelope Fittes, Rhianna Dorris as Kat Godwin, Paddy Holland as Bobby Vernon.

When Netflix launched Lockwood & Co., starring Ruby Stokes, fans of supernatural teen shows were giddy. The program centers on a clairvoyant girl who enlists the help of two adolescent boys who run a ghost-hunting business to battle the dangerous ghosts that are now terrorizing London. The new detective fantasy series Lockwood & Co. is based on the Jonathan Stroud novel series of the same name and is set in a world where psychic teenagers are utilized to combat the paranormal.

The series takes its name from the principal organization it follows: a trio of renegade youths who operate without adult supervision to solve a horrific mystery. In addition to well-known actors like Morven Christie, Ivanno Jeremiah, Lily Newmark, Luke Treadaway, and Ben Crompton, the cast also includes up-and-coming talent. So who else is in the show? Let's find out!

The Cast of Lockwood and Co.: Meet All the Actors and Actresses From the Show!

There are several cast members, so let's have a look at the cast members of Lockwood & Co.

Lucy Carlyle as Ruby Stokes

Ruby Stokes is (@rubystokess), played by cast Lucy Carlyle. She was previously wrongfully accused of being responsible for the deaths of her coworkers. She is an agent with Lockwood & Co. She fled to London and eventually ended up working for the company. When Lucy first comes to Lockwood & Co., she is at her lowest point after experiencing rejection from her family, losing her only friend, and being held accountable for a fatal workplace accident.

Francesca was Ruby Stokes' most well-known role in Bridgerton. She has also appeared in the movies Una, Rocks, and A Banquet. She will play the lead role in the upcoming drama The Burning Girls, which is based on the same-named book.

Cameron Chapman as Anthony Lockwood

Another cast of Lockwood & Co., the first agency in London to conduct services without adult supervision, was founded by Anthony Lockwood, who has experienced trauma in the past. Lockwood is a brilliant teen who is haunted by the death of his parents and has a big ego to prove it. Although he is occasionally irresponsible, he is a fearless leader. Cameron Chapman cast as Anthony is his debut performance at Lockwood & Co.

Ivanno Jeremiah as Inspector Barnes

Callous and tenacious Inspector Barnes oversees the ghost-hunting organizations in London for the Department of Psychical Research and Control, making sure they adhere to the law. Barnes nags Lockwood & Co. about how they conduct business continuously, but in the end, he typically gives them another chance to prove themselves.

Ivanno Jeremiah (@ivannojeremiah_), a British-Ugandan actor, is well known for his appearances in the science fiction series Humans, the fantasy series A Discovery of Witches, and the drama The Flood, in which he co-starred with Lena Headey from Game of Thrones.

Ali Hadji-Heshmati as George Karim

George Karim, who works as the chief researcher at Lockwood & Co., is brilliant and unconventional. He serves as the unmatched researcher for Lockwood & Co. as well as a field agent with a talent for touch. He is obsessed with attempting to understand the causes of the problem since it fascinates him.

Another rising star, Ali Hadji-Heshmati (@alihadjiheshmati), juggles his acting commitments with part-time coursework at the prestigious University of Oxford. He has also appeared in the comedy series Bad Education. In his native UK, he is best known for his performance in the final season of the venerable medical drama Holby City.

Jack Bandeira as Quill Kipps

Quill Kipps, the manager of Fittes Agency, and his crew are Lockwood & Co.'s toughest adversaries. Kipps, a skilled fencer, and ghost hunter are crafty and aggressive; they frequently spar as they compete for the same ghostly jobs. Jack Bandeira may be seen in the Harry Styles movie My Policeman as well as the crime dramas Happy Valley and Silent Witness. He also made an appearance in the Sex Education Netflix series.

Hayley Konadu as Flo Bones

Flo Bones, a former spy, now searches for artifacts on the Thames' mud flats. Flo is Lockwood's buddy and a former colleague who is jaded and cynical. She pursues ghostly items as a relic hunter, a hazardous job that leaves her on the periphery of civilization. When Edmund Bickerstaff's riddle is all but solved by Lockwood & Co., Flo comes in to offer her assistance. In the Marvel television series Moon Knight, Hayley Konadu (@hayleykonadu_) played Tefnut's Avatar.

Morven Christie as Penelope Fittes

Penelope Fittes, the daughter of Marissa Fittes, the agency's founder, serves as its head. The Fittes Agency, George's previous employer and the most reputable paranormal research firm in London, is headed by Penelope Fittes, the founder's granddaughter. She intimidates Lockwood & Co., and their paths still cross, for better or worse.

Scottish actor Morven Christie is well-known for appearing in TV dramas such as The A Word, Grantchester, The Bay, and The Replacement.

Rhianna Dorris as Kat Godwin

Kat Godwin is an agent, working for Fittes and reporting to Quill Kipps. She is Lucy's competitor and a gifted listener who can discern the lingering remnants of a ghost's mental pain. Before Lockwood & Co., Rhianna Dorris (@rhiannadorris) appeared in the movies The Kid Who Would Be King and The Secret Life of Boys, both directed by Joe Cornish.

Paddy Holland as Bobby Vernon

Bobby Vernon is a Fittes agent and researcher who reports to Quill Kipps. He is George's adversary at Lockwood & Co. He is scholarly, sarcastic, and young. Paddy Holland has previously made appearances in the Apple TV+ series Invasion and the Will Ferrell feature Holmes & Watson.
