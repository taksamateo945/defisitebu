Lisa Rinna Rewears 10-Year-Old Dress, Poses With Husband Harry Hamlin

 Posted on January 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  192 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Timeless style!  

Lisa Rinna turned up at the HBO Post Golden Globe Awards party on Sunday, Jan. 11, looking slim as ever in a curve-hugging lace Alaia gown.

But get this: Her black and nude lace frock is the same one the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills‘ newest firecracker, 51, wore almost 10 years ago to the 12th Annual Race to Erase MS event. 

Aside from slimming down, and trading leopard sandals for a basic black pair — Rinna’s killer bod is still in tip-top form. “Here is the full Monty. #alaia and HH in #TomFord,” she captioned a pic describing her eye-popping look on Instagram on Jan. 12.
