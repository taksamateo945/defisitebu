Tallulah Willis Shaved Head After Watching Demi Moore's G.I. Jane

 Posted on May 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  339 words  |  Martina Birk

Most definitely like mother, like daughter. Tallulah Willis revealed in Nylon‘s May 2015 issue that her recent head-shaving experience was, in fact, influenced by her mom Demi Moore‘s famous scene in G.I. Jane.

“I thought, if I could feel beautiful with no hair, then I will literally feel good in any situation,” the 20 year old reflected. “I actually watched G.I. Jane two days before I shaved my head. I don’t think I was 100 percent inspired by it, but I think it may have manifested the idea in my mind.”

In fact, Willis’ head was shaved by her superstar mom this past January. Tallulah shared a video via Instagram of the process, calling it “mommy-daughter bonding activities.”

While she’s now accustomed to the public eye, growing up with celebrity parents was a dramatic experience for Tallulah, as well as her sisters Rumer and Scout. “[There was an emphasis on] being hidden,” Bruce and Demi’s daughter noted. “Not because we had anything to hide, but because my parents wanted to protect us.”

The result? “Now I don’t have a conscious thought process of like, ‘I’m putting something out there that is shocking or not shocking,'” she noted. “I did this video one night and I was looking normal, and then I made this weird face because I thought it was really funny. But then I was like, ‘Damn, I can’t believe Daily Mail just posted that.'”
