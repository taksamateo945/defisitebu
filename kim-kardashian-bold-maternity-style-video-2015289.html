No one knows how to make a strong maternity style statement quite like Kim Kardashian!

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 34, is expecting her second child—a son, due in December—with husband Kanye West, and this time around, she’s honoring her blossoming hourglass shape with bold, fitted styles.

Just some of the highlights? The feathered Roberto Cavalli dress she wore to the CFDA Fashion Awards in June, the khaki-colored Balmain maxi dress she modeled at MTV’s VMAs in August, and the sheer, bump-flaunting Givenchy number she wore to the French house’s fashion week presentation in September.
